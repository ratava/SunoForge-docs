The Chords tab displays the chord chart generated alongside your song.

---

## Core Chords

Each chord is shown as a card with:

- **Chord name** — e.g. `Am`, `Fmaj7`
- **Role** — the chord's function in the key (e.g. *tonic*, *subdominant*, *dominant*)

---

## Progression

The full chord progression for the song, shown as a sequence. A **Copy** button lets you copy it directly to clipboard.

---

## Harmonic Notes

AI-generated commentary on the progression — scale, mood implications, and any notable harmonic choices.

---

The chord chart updates automatically each time a full song is generated. Regenerating an individual section does not update the chord chart.
