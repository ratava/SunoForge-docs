The left panel contains five tabs for configuring your song before generation, plus Song Presets at the bottom of the Settings tab.

<div class="grid grid-header">
  <div class="cell cell--2"><strong>Tab</strong></div>
  <div class="cell cell--auto"><strong>What it does</strong></div>
</div>
<div class="grid">
  <div class="cell cell--2"><a href="/left-panel-settings/">⚙️ Settings</a></div>
  <div class="cell cell--auto">Core song configuration — genre, mood, key, tempo, structure lengths</div>
</div>
<div class="grid">
  <div class="cell cell--2"><a href="/left-panel-vocal/">🎤 Vocal</a></div>
  <div class="cell cell--auto">Lead vocal type, profiles, and choir/backing vocals</div>
</div>
<div class="grid">
  <div class="cell cell--2"><a href="/left-panel-structure/">🎵 Structure</a></div>
  <div class="cell cell--auto">Custom structure builder and genre-specific presets</div>
</div>
<div class="grid">
  <div class="cell cell--2"><a href="/left-panel-sound/">🔊 Sound</a></div>
  <div class="cell cell--auto">Instruments, production style, effects, and influences</div>
</div>
<div class="grid">
  <div class="cell cell--2"><a href="/left-panel-lyrics/">✍️ Lyrics</a></div>
  <div class="cell cell--auto">AI mode and your own lyrics input</div>
</div>
<div class="grid">
  <div class="cell cell--2"><a href="/left-panel-presets/">📌 Song Presets</a></div>
  <div class="cell cell--auto">Save and reload full configuration snapshots</div>
</div>
